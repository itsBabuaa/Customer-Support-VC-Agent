## Technical Support
- Software Issues: OS reinstall guide available on website
- Driver Updates: Download center at novalaptops.com/drivers
- BIOS Updates: Available per model, instructions included
- Common Issues:
  - Won't turn on → Hold power 15 seconds, try different charger
  - Slow performance → Check Task Manager, update drivers, run disk cleanup
  - Wi-Fi issues → Reset network adapter, update Wi-Fi driver
  - Battery draining fast → Check power settings, reduce brightness, update BIOS
  - Screen flickering → Update GPU driver, check refresh rate settings

## Frequently Asked Questions

Q1: What is your best laptop for programming?

Q7: Can I finance my purchase?
A: Yes, 0% APR for 12 months on orders over $500 through Affirm. Apply at checkout.

Q8: What if my laptop arrives damaged?
A: Contact us immediately. We'll arrange free return shipping and send a replacement or full refund.
